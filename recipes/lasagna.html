Microwave Lasagna for 2

Image from Pexels.

Home-cooked. Cheap. Easy. Quick.

Ingredients

  • 90 g uncooked lasagna noodles (about 3 sheets)
  • 300 ml marinara or pasta sauce (1 1/4 cup)
  • 120 g cooked ground beef or veggies (1/2 cup)
  • 240 g quickmelt processed cheese (1 cup)
  • 3 g garlic powder (1/2 teaspoon)
  • 3 g dried basil or oregano (1/2 teaspoon)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 ml canola oil (1 teaspoon)

Prep Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles for 3-4 minutes until slightly softened but still firm. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© ground beef or veggies with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and herbs until cooked through.

Assembly

  1. Spoon a thin layer (~3 tsbp) of sauce on the bottom of a microwave-safe dish(~6x6 inches).
  2. Place parboiled noodle sheets.
  3. Spread half the cooked meat/veggies (~60 g).
  4. Dollop 120 g of cheese evenly.
  5. Add some sauce (~3 tbsp).
  6. Repeat for the second layer.
  7. For the third layer, place noodles, top with remaining sauce and cheese.

Microwave Cooking

Cooking Time ~30 minutes
  1. Cover loosely with a microwave-safe lid or plastic wrap (poke a few holes for steam).
  2. Microwave at 70-80% power for a total of 12-15 minutes.
  3. Check after 10 minutes, rotate the dish if needed to ensure even cooking.
  4. After microwaving, let it rest covered for 5-10 minutes to let the lasagna set and finish cooking gently.

Tips

  • Mix garlic powder and dried herbs into cheese.
  • Drizzle canola oil on top before cooking to help slight browning.
  • Add fresh herbs or chili flakes before serving for extra punch.
